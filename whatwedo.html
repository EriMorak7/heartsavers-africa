What We Do

Free, hands-on CPR training empowering communities across two continents.

Our Training

In-Person CPR Education

We deliver practical, expert-led CPR training sessions designed to equip everyday people with the skills to respond in cardiac emergencies.

Hands-On Training

Our sessions are fully practical and interactive — participants learn by doing, not just watching. Real techniques, real confidence.

Community Impact

We serve the general public and schools in both the United States and Nigeria, reaching communities where CPR education is needed most.

Expert Instructors

Our trainers are NREMT-certified EMTs and CPR professionals with real-world emergency medical experience.

Group Bookings Only

We operate on a group booking model — perfect for schools, churches, community centres, and corporate organizations.

Completely Free

All of our training sessions are offered at absolutely no cost. We believe life-saving education should be accessible to everyone.

Schools Programme

We partner with schools to train students and staff, creating safer learning environments and empowering the next generation.

Important: No Certification Issued

Please note that our training sessions are educational and awareness-focused. No formal CPR certification is issued upon completion of our programme. Our goal is to build awareness, confidence, and basic life-saving skills within communities.
