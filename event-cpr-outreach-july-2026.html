HeartSavers Africa Community CPR Training Outreach @ The International School Ibadan

Ibadan, Nigeria · Event Recap

← Back to Events

HeartSavers Africa recently organized a Community CPR and First Aid Training Outreach aimed at equipping individuals with practical life-saving skills. Participants learned how to recognize emergencies, perform CPR, and provide immediate assistance before professional medical help arrives.

The event brought together students, professionals, and community members who were eager to gain the confidence and knowledge needed to respond effectively during critical situations.

Training Highlights

  • Hands-on CPR demonstrations
  • Basic first aid techniques
  • Emergency response best practices
  • Interactive learning sessions
  • Community engagement and awareness
“Every person trained is another potential lifesaver in their community.”

Impact

Through practical demonstrations and guided exercises, participants gained valuable experience in responding to emergencies. Events like these continue to support HeartSavers Africa's mission of making life-saving knowledge accessible to everyone.
