Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Driving change through education and empowerment in emergency response.

Our Mission

To equip individuals and communities with life-saving CPR skills through accessible, free, and high-quality training — bridging the gap in emergency response education across the U.S. and Nigeria.

Our Vision

A world where every person has the confidence and knowledge to act in a cardiac emergency — where bystanders become lifesavers, and no life is lost due to lack of basic first aid awareness.
